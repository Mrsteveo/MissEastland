CREATING GREATER OPPORTUNITIES

WITH SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURS

Miss Eastlands Social Entrepreneurship is all about making a positive impact. By working with pioneers and pathfinders who use their business as a way to make everyday life betterfor those who need it most, we strive towards a more equal and inclusive society.

WE EMPOWER
YOUNG PEOPLE

FOR BETTER EVERYDAY LIVES

There are millions of people in slums and informal settlements of Nairobi’s Eastlands whose lives are a constant struggle to provide for themselves and their families. By backing and working with social entrepreneurs, we create new opportunities and contribute to better everyday lives around Eastlands.

Together with dedicated partners and experienced Miss Eastlands Organization’s co-workers, we develop programmes, products and services to scale up social entrepreneurs' businesses and increase their impact.

PARTNERSHIPS

Different Kinds Of Partnerships

We team up with donors, corporates, business, individuals and like-minded stakeholders as well as community-based organizations to support established social entrepreneurs to increase their impact.

We collaborate with social entrepreneurs to produce unique items and develop services for Miss Eastlands Organization – that way we contribute to creating jobs for those who need it the most.

WHERE WE OPERATE

_________

Exclusion and inequality are global challenges. But we must solve it at local and community levels. Our ambition is to operate in all slums and informal settlements of Nairobi’s Eastlands region to create a better everyday life for as many people as possible.

SOCIAL ENTREPRENEUR STORIES
__________

Improving Young Mothers’ Living Conditions In Slums And Informal Settlements Of Nairobi’s Eastlands

One of the social entrepreneurs we collaborate with is Pamela Gatwiri of PAM NAIL LACQUE. Through her business Pam Nail Polish, she trains young and vulnerable lactating mothers who want to work in the beauty sector with the aim to learn, develop and practice as required by the market. The emphasis is put on the trainee to acquire the ability to perform as confident and competent beautician. There are thousands of young mothers in slums living in poor conditions which pose health risks. Read full story

THIS IS HOW WE WORK
__________

PARTNERSHIP THAT INSPIRE CHANGE

Our work is about inspiring and engaging others to make a change in the community at local level. It’s a collaboration between us, social entrepreneurs-corporates,individuals, and civil society organisations. By partnering with social entrepreneurs and connecting them with those in need we create real and sustainable opportunities. To highlight the equal partnering, we focus on mutual learning and build on each other’s strengths. We look at how to use our knowledge and experience to help both the people we serve and social entrepreneurs grow and have a bigger impact in the community-it’s all about win-win!

THE APPROACH TO ACHIEVE OUR GOALS
__________

Empowerment

We focus on empowerment of vulnerable and marginalised people and the support of social entrepreneurs and other partners. We believe this is the only way to contribute to significant and lasting change.

Innovation

We work with piloting and continuous innovation, both by finding new solutions with partners and supporting the activities of social entrepreneurs.

Lead By Example

We lead by what we do and by sharing our learnings to enable others to advance the social entrepreneurship agenda.

Co-Creation

By co-creating, we find new ways of working, discover synergies and encourage learning and sharing of competencies.

Trust

Building strong and long-lasting relationships internally and externally is crucial to maximise our outcome.

Transparency

We are open about both positive and negative experiences, learnings and results and strive to be clear about expectations, division of responsibilities and labour, and how to solve issues that we may encounter on the way, both internally and externally.
