LET'S PARTNER

PROVIDING OPPORTUNITIES

We partner with social enterprises committed to elevating the living standards of vulnerable youth and providing opportunities for youth and young at most risk lactating mothers in need. We believe that social entrepreneurs should have access to all the resources they need to help these youth succeed, including facilitating entrepreneurial skills training, providing loans and grants, market linkages and other essentials. When you buy from Miss EstlandSmile online shop, your purchase helps others who are also making a difference in the lives of vulnerable youth, young at most risk lactating mothers and their children.

EVERY GIFT YOU PURCHASE GIVES BACK TO YOUNG AT MOST RISK MOTHERS AND THEIR CHILDREN

OUR PEOPLE

COMFORT. COMPASSION. CONSCIOUSNESS.

Together, we believe we can make a difference in the world by making responsible, sustainable design and supporting a mission that’s close to our hearts. Reducing the incidences of rampant newborn dumping is a big task, but we’ve committed to curbing the rates of infant dumping by working with our partners at Miss Eastlands Organization. Over these past five years, we have been inspired by the support of our community and know together we can make a difference. Change is within reach!
