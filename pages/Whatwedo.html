WHAT WE DO

WHAT WE DO
__________

The MEO team is a collection of experienced professionals and empowered youth leaders. We are united by our passion and conviction that educating, empowering and engaging young people is key to sustainable and equitable development in Eastlands.

Educate

We provide training and education to promising disadvantaged youth and at most risk young lactating mothers.

Cloud

Engage

We engage young people and vulnerable lactating mothers through youth-led sports programs.

Empower

We empower young people as well as young and vulnerable lactating mothers to advocate for themselves and make healthy decisions for their futures.

Lead

We develop young people’s leadership and capacity to drive change in their families and communities.
