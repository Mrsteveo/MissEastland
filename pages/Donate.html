OUR GOALS

A SUITABLE LIVING ENVIRONMENT – which includes:

o Improving the safety and livability of neighbourhoods
o Reducing the isolation of income groups within the neighborhoods through job creation and retention for youth, and the revitalization of dead businesses due to COVID-19 pandemic.

EXPANDED ECONOMIC OPPORTUNITIES- which includes:

o Job creation and retention;
o Establishment, stabilization and expansion of small businesses (including micro-businesses);.
o The provision of jobs to vulnerable youth living in slums and informal settlements.
o Access to capital and credit for development activities that promote the long-term economic and social viability of the community;
o Empowerment and self-sufficiency for vulnerable youth to reduce generational poverty. o Empowerment and self-sufficiency for vulnerable youth to reduce generational poverty.

Become part of Lifeline, a recurring giving program that helps vulnerable youth — especially young at most risk lactating mothers — get the training they need to start savings-led microfinance groups.

Youth in urban poor communities have little to no access to formal credit or savings services. Without these services, they are held back from growing businesses and saving money. But you can be their Lifeline—and help them get the training they need to start savings groups. It’s such a simple solution—and makes such a big social and economic impact. Live your faith in an extraordinary way—become a Lifeline member today. Our microfinance programs target 2,000 youth yearly.

When you join Lifeline, you will…

o Uplift young at most risk lactating mothers’ families and communities
o Live your faith in an extraordinary way
o Keep Miss Eastlands Organization running effectively and efficiently

THE APPROACH TO ACHIEVE OUR GOALS

DONATE YOUR TIME AND TALENT

Your time and skill are invaluable – particularly if you are involved in the social entrepreneurship. We need all sorts of volunteers but trainings and education for savings and internal lending communities and areas where you can save us money are all particularly appreciated.

PROGRAMME SPONSORSHIP

If you are part of a local business that is looking for charitable cause to support, what better cause than empowering vulnerable youth? For information on business sponsorship get in touch today!
