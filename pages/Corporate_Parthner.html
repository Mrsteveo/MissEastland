WHY PARTNER

GOODS FOR THE GREATER GOOD

Our mission is to close the need gap. As a leader in product sourcing and purposeful giving, as well as fundraising, we partner with some of the world’s largest corporations to source essential goods and distribute them to the people we serve, supporting people in need to open opportunities for them.
